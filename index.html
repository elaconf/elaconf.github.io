About

ELA Conf is a conference with a goal to create a safe, supportive, inspiring and comfortable community for adult women (18+) to gain the confidence needed to become leaders, speakers, and teachers in the world of tech. All profits from this event will be donated to Girl Develop It.

In an industry where women are so often marginalized, leadership skills are crucial to making an impact in the field and creating diversity in the workplace. Companies with greater top-level gender diversity perform better financially, are more empathetic, and more innovative. When women become leaders, the entire tech community benefits.

Our code of conduct is the heart of our event. Please reach out to us if you have any concerns or there is something you would like to see included that isnâ€™t.

What’s included

  • Meals & snacks — We'll have snacks on Friday evening and on Saturday we'll serve breakfast, lunch, coffee, and even more snacks.
  • Lightning talks — Join us Friday night for fun, casual lightning talks.
  • Meet & greet — We want to make sure there are as few unfamilar faces at the conf as possible!
  • Networking — We'll wrap up the conference on Saturday afternoon with a networking event.
